Common Questions

Frequently asked.

How long does a typical residential project take?+

Depending on scale, a residential project usually runs 6-14 months from concept design to handover, including statutory approvals.

Do you handle government approvals and permits?+

Yes — our project management service includes liaison for building permits, environmental clearances and occupancy certificates.

Can you work with an existing structural consultant?+

Absolutely. We frequently collaborate with clients' existing structural, MEP and landscape consultants.

What's your fee structure?+

Fees are typically a percentage of project cost or a fixed fee for defined scopes — we'll share a clear proposal after the first consultation.
